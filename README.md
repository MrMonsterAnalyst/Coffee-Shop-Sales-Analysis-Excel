#Coffee Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)
Project Summary

This project is an interactive Excel dashboard built to analyze coffee shop sales data and extract actionable business insights.
It demonstrates strong hands-on skills in Excel-based data analysis, dashboard design, and business reporting without using VBA or external tools.

The dashboard enables stakeholders to quickly evaluate sales performance, customer behavior, product contribution, and store-level efficiency.

#Business Questions Addressed

-> How much revenue is generated overall and per customer?

-> When do customers place the most orders during the day?

-> Which product categories and items drive the highest sales?

-> How do different store locations perform in terms of sales and footfall?

-> How does customer traffic vary by weekday?

-> What order sizes are most common?

#Key Metrics (KPIs)

-> Total Sales

-> Total Footfall

-> Average Bill per Person

-> Average Orders per Person

All KPIs update dynamically based on selected filters.

Dashboard Components
Sales by Hour

Identifies peak and low-demand hours

Useful for workforce and operational planning

Category-wise Sales Contribution (%)

Shows revenue distribution across product categories

Helps identify primary revenue drivers

Order Size Distribution

Analyzes customer purchasing behavior (Small, Regular, Large)

Useful for pricing and bundling strategies

Store Location Performance

Compares sales and footfall across multiple store locations

Highlights high-performing and underperforming stores

Top 5 Products by Sales

Identifies best-selling products

Supports inventory and promotion decisions

Orders by Weekday

Analyzes customer traffic trends throughout the week

Useful for campaign and staffing decisions

Interactivity & Filtering
